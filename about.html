About Us

Family-Owned Since 1981

Stephen's Asphalt Paving started when Thomas Stephen bought a used paver and began doing driveways around Massillon. That was 1981. Forty-five years later, the company is run by his son Evan - and the same name is on the truck.

This is a family operation. Evan grew up around asphalt - on job sites after school, riding along in the truck during summers, learning to run equipment before he could drive a car. When Thomas was ready to step back, Evan didn't have to learn the business. He already knew it.

That matters because it means you're not dealing with a startup figuring things out. Every crew member knows what they're doing. The equipment is owned, not rented. And when you call, you're talking to the owner - not a call center.

"We're not the biggest paving company in Stark County. But we do every job like our name's on the line - because it is."

We work across Massillon, Canton, North Canton, Jackson Township, Perry Township, Canal Fulton, and the surrounding area. Residential and commercial. Small driveway patches and full parking lot repaves. If it's asphalt, we do it.

Stephen's Asphalt is fully equipped and insured. We own our own pavers, rollers, and trucks. We don't subcontract. The crew that shows up is our crew, and they've done this before.

Forty-five years in business means something. It means we've seen every kind of driveway, every kind of soil condition, every Ohio winter's worth of damage. There's no situation we haven't handled. And there's a reason our customers come back - and send their neighbors our way.

What We Do

Paving, Repairs, & Sealcoating

Everything your driveway or parking lot needs, from fresh asphalt to annual maintenance.

Residential Driveways

Whether it's a 20-foot pad or a winding 200-foot drive, we grade it, base it, and pave it so it lasts. We've done everything from ranch-house driveways in Massillon to long private drives out past Canal Fulton.

Every driveway starts with proper grading and a compacted stone base. We use hot-mix asphalt and our own rollers to get a smooth, even finish. Most residential jobs are done in a day or two - not a week.

We serve homeowners across Massillon, Canton, North Canton, Jackson Township, Perry Township, and the surrounding Stark County area.

Sealcoating

A fresh sealcoat does two things: it protects the asphalt from water and sun damage, and it makes your driveway look brand new. We recommend sealing every two to three years to get the most life out of your pavement.

We use commercial-grade coal tar emulsion sealer applied by hand with squeegees - not sprayed from a truck. That means we work it into every crack and edge, and you get full, even coverage every time.

Sealcoating is the most affordable way to maintain your driveway. It costs a fraction of repaving and adds years to the surface life.

Repairs & Patching

Not every driveway needs a full repave. Sometimes you just need to fix a pothole before it gets worse, seal up cracks before water gets underneath, or put down an overlay on a surface that's still structurally sound.

We do all of it:

  • Pothole repair and filling
  • Crack sealing
  • Infrared patching for seamless repairs
  • Asphalt overlays and resurfacing

The key with repairs is catching them early. A small crack that costs $200 to seal today turns into a $4,000 repave next year if water gets underneath and freezes over a couple Ohio winters.

Commercial & Parking Lots

We pave and maintain parking lots for businesses, churches, apartment complexes, and industrial properties across Stark County. Whether you need a new lot paved from scratch or your existing lot patched and resealed, we handle it.

Commercial work is about minimizing disruption. We work around your schedule, section off areas so your business stays open, and get the job done fast. We have the equipment for large-scale jobs - this isn't a residential crew trying to do commercial work.
